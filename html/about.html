Who Am I?

My journey into the world of web development was anything but conventional. A civil engineer by training, I started to more actively explore my interest in programming in the summer of 2019. One chapter into Eric Matthes' Python Crash Course, I realized I was hooked.

One month later, my first website went live.

Core Competencies

I'm experienced in the use of MVC frameworks to deliver performative web applications to clients of all types. I am a firm believer in using the right tool for the right job, and abhor tools that force me to reinvent the wheel.

My greatest areas of strength are:

  • Databases: PostgresQL, MySQL, Redis
  • Frameworks/Runtimes: Rails, Django, Node.js
  • API Standards: REST, Graphql
  • Javascript: React, jQuery, StimulusJS
  • Devops: Terraform, AWS
  • General Tools: Git, Docker, VSCode
