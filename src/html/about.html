Who Am I?

My journey into the world of web development was anything but conventional. As I neared the end of my degree in Civil Engineering, I couldn't escape the feeling that there was something else I'd rather be doing.

Though I'd been in love with computers since I was a child, the idea of learning how to TELL them what to do intimidated me. Nevertheless, I decided to throw myself into it headfirst. Two months later, my first website was born.
